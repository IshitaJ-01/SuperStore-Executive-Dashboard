# SuperStore-Executive-Dashboard

📁Project Title: Global Superstore Executive Dashboard

🧰Tools: Power BI, DAX, Power Query.

🔑​Key Features:
 - ​Regional Performance Slicers: Interactive top-bar navigation for Asia Pacific, Europe, LATAM, and USCA.
​
 - Category Decomposition: A donut chart combined with stacked bar charts to show Sales by Category vs. Year.

 - ​Dynamic Ticker: A bottom-scroll style metric bar showing specific sales figures for Binders, Paper, Phones, etc.

 - Customer Insights: Ranking of the Top 5 customers by revenue (e.g., Aaron Bergman at $0.55M).
​

🔑Key Metrics Visualized:
​
 - Total Sales Growth: Trended from $11M (2012) to $21M (2015).
​Market Leader: Asia Pacific ($20.08M in Sales).

 - Product Leader: Technology (Highest revenue share).
